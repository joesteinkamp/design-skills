# Portfolio Evaluation Template

> **Field Definitions** — use for output validation.
>
> | Field | Required | Type | Validation |
> |-------|----------|------|------------|
> | Role | yes | text | target role title |
> | Level | yes | text | target level, e.g. mid / senior / staff / manager |
> | Source | yes | enum | URL / PDF / pasted |
> | Rubric used | yes | enum | default / custom |
> | Summary | yes | text | 2-3 sentences covering overall read against the target level |
> | Recommendation | yes | enum | advance / hold / decline |
> | Recommendation rationale | yes | text | tied to level calibration |
> | Rubric Scorecard | yes | list | one row per rubric dimension |
> | Dimension | yes | text | rubric dimension name |
> | Score | yes | enum | 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 or unproven |
> | Level vs target | yes | enum | meets / exceeds / below / unproven |
> | Evidence citation | yes | text | specific reference to a case study or artifact |
> | Case Study Notes | yes | list | min 1 case study |
> | Role clarity | yes | enum | clear / partial / unclear |
> | Problem | yes | text | the problem the case study frames |
> | Process | yes | text | how the candidate worked |
> | Outcome | yes | text | result and evidence of impact |
> | Strongest evidence | yes | text | best signal in the case study |
> | Weakest link | yes | text | thinnest part of the case study |
> | Strengths | yes | list | min 2, each with supporting evidence |
> | Gaps & Risks | yes | list | min 1; includes unproven dimensions and unclear attribution |
> | Interview Probes | yes | list | min 3, each targeting a named gap |
> | Open Questions | yes | list | min 1; what the portfolio could not answer |
